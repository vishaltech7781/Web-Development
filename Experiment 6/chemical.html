Description:

Methane (CH4): The simplest alkane, a major component of natural gas used as a fuel.

Ethane (C2H6): A colorless gas used in the petrochemical industry to produce ethylene.

Propane (C3H8): Commonly used as a fuel for heating, cooking, and in portable stoves.

Butane (C4H10): Found in lighter fluid and used in camping fuel and aerosol sprays.

Pentane (C5H12): Used as a solvent and in fuel blending for gasoline.

Hexane (C6H14): A solvent in industries, mainly for oil extraction from seeds.

Heptane (C7H16): Used in laboratories and as a fuel additive.

Octane (C8H18): A key component in gasoline, known for its octane rating in fuels.

Nonane (C9H20): Found in diesel fuel and industrial solvents.

Decane (C10H22): Used as a fuel additive and in jet fuel formulations.
